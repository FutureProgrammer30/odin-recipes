Lava Cake

Description

A chocolate sponge cake with a runny chocolate liquid in the center.

Ingredients

  • 6 ½ (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • ½ cup butter, cut into 8 pieces
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Grease and flour four 6-ounce ramekins
  3. Place chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler over simmering water
  4. Stir frequently, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching, until chocolate is melted, about 5 minutes
  5. Combine eggs and sugar in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until pale and thick, about 5 minutes
  6. Mix in melted chocolate, flour, and cocoa powder until combined
  7. Pour batter into the prepared ramekins and place on a baking tray
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the sides are set but the middle is still jiggly, 10 to 13 minutes
  9. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving
