Banana Bread

Description

This is a sweet bread made of mashed bananas

Ingredients

  • flour
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • banana

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven
  5. Bake for at least an hour
  6. Insert a toothpick into the center of the loaf and check if it's clean
  7. When it is clean, take it out of the oven and let it set for 10 min
  8. Turn it out of the pan and enjoy!!!
